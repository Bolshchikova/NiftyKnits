Toys

Handmade toy can be a wonderful gift or a perfect complement to your interior, or a part of a collection. Each toy has its own personality and its look depends on the used yarn and stuff. So it is impossible to make an exact copy. All toys contain small parts, not suitable for children under 3 years old.

Timmy the Hedgehog

Timmy loves summer very much.

This cute toy is hand crocheted from soft acrylic yarn and stuffed with non-allergenic polyester fiberfill.

The thorns are knitted from soft eyelash yarn. The paws are reinforced with the wire inside.

All parts are securely sewn to the body except the scarf, the mushroom and the chamomile.

Size: 20 cm (8") tall.

Pattern by Torisa

Size: 20 cm (8") when seated,
full length 32 cm (12.75").

Pattern by Elena Gunger.
