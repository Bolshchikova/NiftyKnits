Shawls

An exquisite hand knitted shawl makes your outfit unique and you will be irresistible. Besides, it will keep you warm. Each shawl is unique. Even being made of the same yarn and with the same tools,  the shawls will be different.

On the Wings of Love

This beautiful shawl is hand-knitted using design by Alla Omelyanets and made of lace yarn (45% silk, 55% superwash merino).

Weight approx. 90 g plus 30 g of Japanese Toho seed beads (over 1200 pieces).

Measurements: 64 cm (25") along spine & 140 cm (55") across top.

Magic Bruges Lace

The luxurious stole is crocheted with cobweb yarn (cotton, viscose, angora, cashmere).

It took me over 1650 m (1 mile) of yarn to create the stole.

Weight approx. 110 g.

Measurements: 54 cm (21.5") width, 172 cm (67.75") length.

Dahlia

The elegant soft shawl is hand knitted with lace yarn  (superkid mohair, wool, polyamide).

Weight approx. 100 g plus 20 g of Japanese Toho seed beads (over 800 pieces).

Measurements: 60 cm (23.5") along spine & 132 cm (52") across top.

Trachtentuch by Erich Engeln

The warm soft shawl is hand knitted with LINEAPIU Camelot yarn (superkid mohair, wool, polyamide).

Weight approx. 45 g.

Measurements: 55 cm (21.6") along spine & 130 cm (51") across top.

Frosty Gossamer

The stole is crocheted with CEAM Merino Extra Fine yarn.

It took me 3750 m (2.3 miles) of yarn to create the stole.

Weight approx. 250 g (8.8 oz).

Measurements: 50 cm (20") width, 175 cm (69") length without fringes.

Queen Silvia

This tender stole is made in the traditional Estonian style. The body of the shawl has many nupps (bobbles) that gives the lace dignity and also proofs that this shawl is handknitted.

It took me 1750 m of viscose/kid-mohair yarn to create the stole.

Weight approx. 90 g.

Measurements: 65 cm (25.5") width, 185 cm (73") length.

Tara

This mysterious shawl is hand knitted with 100% Indian Silk.

It took me 1000 m of silk yarn to create the shawl.

Weight approx. 200 g.

Measurements: 64 cm (25") along spine & 132 cm (52") across top.

White Peacock

This exquisite elegant stole is hand knitted with Scrumptious lace yarn (45% silk, 55% superwash merino).

It took me over 1100 m of yarn to create the stole.

Weight approx. 110 g.

Measurements: 55 cm (21.6") width, 165 cm (65") length.

Snowdrop

This gorgeous shawl is hand knitted with Inka Baby Silk yarn (30% silk, 70% baby Alpaca).

Weight approx. 90 g plus 30 g of Japanese Toho seed beads (over 1200 pieces).

Measurements: 68 cm (27") along spine & 140 cm (55") across top.

Log Cabin Comfort Throw

No, no! It's not a shawl!

This cozy throw is made of blended yarn (20% Superwash Wool and 80% Acrylic) and consists of 72 squares.

Weight approx. 2,3 kg (5.3 lb).

Measurements: 156 cm (61.5") width,  180 cm (70.8") length.
