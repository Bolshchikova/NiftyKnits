Hi, I am Olga. I live in Sunnyvale (San Francisco Bay Area, CA) and I am a fan of the knitting!

Over 35 years ago my mom gave me the ball of a yarn, knitting needles and showed me how to knit some stitches. Thanks to her an incredible exciting knitted world was opened to me. I love the magic of knitting: when from an ordinary ball of yarn a beautiful shawl or a fun toy is created. For the shawls I use cotton, wool, silk yarns, or their combinations.
For the toys I pick a soft yarn pleasant to touch. I am happily married and have 3 wonderful children for whom I like to knit some sweaters or scarves so they do not feel lonely and cold in their adult life. I also love a home cosiness, flowers, singing with guitar, reading about world history and culture, and passionately love traveling!

If you would like to order a shawl or a toy, please feel free to send me a request to olga at bolshchikov dot net

Please allow 2 weeks for each item. I could be done sooner, but that would give me ample time.
